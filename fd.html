What is FD Calculator?

An FD Calculator helps investors estimate the maturity value and interest earned on Fixed Deposits. It provides a quick way to understand how much your investment will grow over time.

Benefits of FD Calculator

  • Instant maturity value calculation
  • Estimate interest earnings
  • Compare different FD tenures
  • Plan safe investments
  • Useful for retirement planning

How FD Works

A Fixed Deposit (FD) allows you to invest a lump sum amount with a bank or financial institution for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit is a secure investment option offered by banks where money is invested for a fixed tenure and earns guaranteed interest.

Is FD safe?

FDs are generally considered one of the safest investment options available.

Can FD be withdrawn before maturity?

Yes, most banks allow premature withdrawal, but a penalty may apply.

What is GST Calculator?

A GST Calculator helps calculate Goods and Services Tax (GST) by adding or removing GST from a base amount. It is useful for businesses, traders, accountants and consumers.

Benefits of GST Calculator

  • Instant GST calculation
  • Add GST quickly
  • Remove GST accurately
  • Useful for invoicing and billing
  • Reduces calculation mistakes

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax. It is an indirect tax levied on goods and services in India.

Can I remove GST using this calculator?

Yes. Select "Remove GST" to calculate the original amount before GST.

Can businesses use this calculator?

Yes. It is useful for businesses, traders, freelancers and consumers.

What is EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator helps you calculate the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) payable towards a loan. It instantly calculates your monthly EMI, total interest payable and total repayment amount.

Benefits of EMI Calculator

  • Instant EMI calculation
  • Compare loan options easily
  • Plan your monthly budget
  • Know total interest payable
  • Make informed financial decisions

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1 + R)^N] ÷ [(1 + R)^N − 1]

  • P = Principal Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Number of Monthly Installments

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount paid every month towards loan repayment.

Can I calculate Home Loan EMI?

Yes. This calculator works for Home Loans, Car Loans, Personal Loans and Business Loans.

Does EMI change every month?

For fixed-rate loans, EMI remains constant. For floating-rate loans, EMI may change depending on interest rate revisions.

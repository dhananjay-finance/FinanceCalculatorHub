What is PF Calculator?

A PF Calculator helps employees estimate the growth of their Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) savings over time. It calculates contributions made by both employee and employer and estimates retirement corpus.

Benefits of PF Calculator

  • Estimate retirement savings
  • Track EPF growth
  • Understand employer contribution
  • Plan long-term finances
  • Visualize retirement corpus

How EPF Works

EPF is a government-backed retirement savings scheme where both employee and employer contribute a fixed percentage of salary every month.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EPF?

Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) is a retirement benefit scheme managed by EPFO in India.

Who contributes to PF?

Both employee and employer contribute a percentage of the employee's salary every month.

Can PF be withdrawn?

Yes, PF can be withdrawn according to EPFO withdrawal rules and eligibility conditions.

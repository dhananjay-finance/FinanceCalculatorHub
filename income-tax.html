What is Income Tax Calculator?

An Income Tax Calculator helps estimate the amount of tax payable on annual income. It is useful for salaried employees, professionals, business owners and taxpayers planning their finances.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Income Tax?

Income Tax is a direct tax imposed by the government on income earned by individuals and businesses.

Can this calculator be used for tax planning?

Yes, it provides a quick estimate of tax liability and helps with financial planning.

Is the result exact?

This calculator provides an estimate. Actual tax liability may vary based on deductions, exemptions and government rules.

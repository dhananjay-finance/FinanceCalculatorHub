What is SIP Calculator?

A SIP Calculator helps investors estimate the future value of their investments made through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). It uses monthly investment amount, expected returns and investment duration to calculate future wealth.

Benefits of SIP Calculator

  • Estimate future wealth creation
  • Understand power of compounding
  • Plan financial goals effectively
  • Compare investment scenarios
  • Improve long-term financial planning

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP stands for Systematic Investment Plan. It allows investors to invest a fixed amount regularly into mutual funds.

Is SIP better than lump sum investment?

SIP helps average out market volatility and is suitable for regular investors.

Can SIP create wealth?

Yes. Long-term SIP investments can generate substantial wealth through compounding.
